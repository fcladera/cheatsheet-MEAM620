\section{Optical Flow}
$\vec{p} = \dfrac{1}{Z} A(\vec{p}) V + B(\vec{p}) \Omega$\\
where \\
$A(\vec{p}) = \begin{pmatrix}
  -1 & 0 & p_x \\ 0 & -1 & p_y \\ 0 & 0 & 0
\end{pmatrix}$\\
$B(\vec{p}) = \begin{pmatrix}
  -p_x p_y & p_x^2 -1 & p_y \\
  1- p_y^2 & p_x p_y & -p_x \\
  0 & 0 6
\end{pmatrix}$\\
$V$: velocity in \alert{inertial frame}\\
$\Omega$: ang velocity in \alert{intertial frame}
